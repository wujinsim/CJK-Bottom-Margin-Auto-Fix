# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

CJK Bottom Margin Auto Fix is an UXP plugin for Adobe InDesign.

## Data Collection

CJK Bottom Margin Auto Fix does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

The plugin works locally inside Adobe InDesign. It reads layout-related information from the currently open InDesign document, such as page size, margins, text frame information, font size, leading, and line count, only for the purpose of calculating and applying bottom margin values.

This information is processed locally and is not sent to the developer or to any third-party service.

## Network Access

The plugin may open external links selected by the user, such as Adobe Exchange or the mulgogi website, from the plugin information screen.

The plugin does not use network access to collect analytics, user data, document data, or personal information.

## Payment

If the plugin is purchased through Adobe Marketplace, payment and licensing are handled by Adobe Marketplace and/or FastSpring. The developer does not directly collect or process payment information through the plugin.

## Third-Party Services

CJK Bottom Margin Auto Fix does not require login to any third-party service.
